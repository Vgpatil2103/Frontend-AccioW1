The Beagle is a breed of small scent hound, similar in appearnce in apperance to the much lrager foxhound. The beagle was developed primarily for hunting hare,known as beagling. Possessing a great sense of smell and superior tracking instincts, the beagle is the primary breed used as a detection dog for prohibited agricultural omports and fooodstufs in quarntine around the world. The beagle is intelligent and is a popular pet due to its suze, good temper, and a lack of inherited health problems
